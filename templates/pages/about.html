{# Our Story Section #}

Why we built this

Graduating is supposed to be the start of something. But the job market doesn't always feel that way. Most job boards mix entry-level roles with positions that require five or ten years of experience — making it genuinely hard to find what you're actually eligible for.

Job Foundry Hub exists to fix that. We focus exclusively on roles suited to graduates and early-career professionals, so the time you spend searching is time spent on opportunities that are actually within reach.

We curate listings across technology, marketing, finance, HR, data, design, and more — covering major UK cities as well as remote positions. Every listing is reviewed to ensure it meets our entry-level criteria before it appears on the platform.

Alongside job listings, we publish practical career guides written by our editorial team to help you with CVs, interviews, salary negotiation, and navigating your first role.
